Consultation and planning:
This involves understanding the client's needs, preferences, and budget, as well as conducting a thorough assessment of the space. It's important to establish clear goals and develop a design plan based on the client's requirements.
Concept Development and Design:
Creating a concept and design scheme that aligns with the client's vision. This includes selecting colors, materials, furniture, and decorative elements that create a cohesive look and feel for the space..
Execution and Implementation:
Once the design plan is finalized, the next step is to bring the vision to life. This involves procuring necessary materials, coordinating with contractors and suppliers, overseeing construction or renovation work, and ensuring that the design is implemented according to the specifications.
Finishing and Styling:
The final step involves adding finishing touches to the space, including accessorizing, styling, and arranging furniture and decor items. This step aims to enhance the overall aesthetics and create a welcoming and functional environment.
